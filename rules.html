Rules and Regulations

General Rules

  • Respectful Conduct: Users must treat each other with respect and courtesy. No harassment, bullying, or discriminatory language will be tolerated.
  • Privacy: Do not share personal information of others without their consent. Respect the privacy of all users.
  • Accountability: Users are responsible for their own actions and content they post.
  • Legal Compliance: All activities on the website must comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Prohibited Content: The following types of content are prohibited:
    • Illegal or harmful content
    • Hate speech or discrimination
    • Spam or unsolicited advertising
    • Sexually explicit material
    • Violent or graphic content
    • Copyright infringement

Specific Regulations

  1. Account Registration:
    • Users must provide accurate information when registering an account.
    • Accounts are for personal use only and cannot be shared or sold.
  2. Content Posting:
    • Users must own the rights to any content they post.
    • Content must be relevant to the website's purpose.
    • No spamming or flooding the website with irrelevant posts.
  3. Communication:
    • Use appropriate language in all communications.
    • No impersonation of other users or individuals.
    • Report any abusive or inappropriate behavior.
  4. Intellectual Property:
    • Respect the intellectual property rights of others.
    • Do not post copyrighted material without permission.
  5. Termination:
    • The website reserves the right to terminate accounts that violate these rules and regulations.
  6. Ride Booking:
    • Only users **above 65 years of age** are eligible to book rides.
